Taller de Tapas Rambla Catalunya

With a design by the famous Lázaro Rosa-Violán, the 'Taller' located on Rambla Catalunya is one of the most attractive restaurants by Taller de Tapas family, which has six restaurants located in the most emblematic points of the city of Barcelona.

It is the ideal place to enjoy a glass of cava in the afternoon, some tapas at any time of the day or a lunch or dinner based on traditional cuisine. The offer also includes burgers, paella, sangria, good wines and cocktails.

Location: Rambla Catalunya

Dining room capaciy: Up to 95 pax

Price Level: Medium

Style: Asian and Mediterranean

Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: No
